Dt. Usha Harish

Dt. Usha Harish has been working as a clinical nutritionist for over 20 years. She specialises in weight loss and treating obesity. She has completed her B.Sc, M.sc in Food and Nutrition, and CFN from Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU).

  • News Channels on TV
